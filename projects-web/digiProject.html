Description

The goal of this initiative is to create a Sri Lankan marketplace where digital media producers and consumers may meet and transact. For this reason, a website and mobile application are being developed. They can construct their own customer and creator profiles in the marketplace. Creators can include information about their current and prior projects, as well as their professional backgrounds and accomplishments. A wide range of options are available to buyers. Service requests or queries can be made by the customers themselves and the producers can put together a customized package of services. Customers can place orders on the company's website or mobile app. Payments can be made using the website or the mobile app. Instant message is available through the website and mobile app. Customers are able to make revives and rate the services provided by the creators. And I would like to add more functions to the system in the future.

Aim

The aim of this project is to create a Sri Lankan marketplace for digital media creators to introduce their services to clients and it helps clients to find creators, services and connect with each other in one place. On Other hand, it helps to consult, optimize, advertise, and promote brands, services, and many other things to target audiences and reach more people. Reduce spending money on paid advertising platforms and reduce the intermediates partnerships and services.
